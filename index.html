The Gambit Roll

An exciting digital dice game for 2 players

Game Overview

This is a digital game where two players take turns rolling a virtual die, trying to be the first to reach 100 points. Player 1 always starts the game.

Objective

Be the first player to reach or exceed 100 points in your total score.

How to Play

Turn Structure

  1. On your turn, you can:
    • Click "Roll" to roll the virtual die
    • Click "Hold" to add your current turn's points to your total score
  2. When you roll the die:
    • If you roll 2-6: That number is added to your Current Score
    • If you roll 1: You lose all points from this turn and your turn ends
  3. After each successful roll (2-6), you must choose:
    • Roll Again: Try to accumulate more points (but risk losing them)
    • Hold: Add your Current Score to your Total Score and end your turn

Turn Example

Player 1's Turn:
  • Rolls a 3 (Current Score = 3)
  • Rolls a 5 (Current Score = 8)
  • Rolls a 6 (Current Score = 14)
  • Clicks "Hold"
  • 14 points are added to Total Score
  • Turn passes to Player 2

Losing Your Turn

If you roll a 1:

  • Your Current Score becomes 0
  • You lose all points accumulated during this turn
  • Your turn ends immediately
  • Play passes to the other player

Winning the Game

  • The game ends immediately when a player's Total Score reaches or exceeds 100 points
  • That player is declared the winner

Strategic Tips

  • Each roll is a risk vs. reward decision
  • The more points you accumulate in your Current Score, the more you risk losing
  • Consider playing more conservatively when you're ahead
  • Take more risks when you're behind

Quick Reference

  • Player 1 always starts first
  • Target Score: 100 points
  • Lose turn: Roll a 1
  • Turn options: Roll Again or Hold
  • Game ends: When a player reaches 100+ points
